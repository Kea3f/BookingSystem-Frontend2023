Lash Extension Aftercare

Following these aftercare tips will help maintain the beauty and longevity of your lash extensions

1. Keep Them Dry: Avoid water, steam, or excessive humidity for the first 24-48 hours after application to let the adhesive fully bond.

2. Gentle Cleansing: Cleanse your lashes daily using an oil-free lash cleanser or a gentle foaming cleanser. Gently brush through them with a spoolie brush to remove any debris or makeup residue.

3. Be Gentle: Avoid rubbing or pulling at your lashes. Instead, pat them dry after cleansing and avoid sleeping on your face to prevent unnecessary friction.

4. Avoid Oil-Based Products: Oil can break down the adhesive, so steer clear of oil-based makeup or skincare products around the eye area.

5. Brush Regularly: Use a clean lash wand to brush through your extensions daily, keeping them aligned and preventing any tangling.

6. Maintain Refills: To keep your lashes looking full and beautiful, schedule regular refill appointments every 2-3 weeks.

7. Protect from Heat: Avoid exposure to excessive heat sources, such as saunas or tanning beds, as heat can weaken the lash adhesive.

8. Be Mindful with Mascara: If using mascara, opt for a water-based, lash extension-friendly formula, and only apply it to the tips, avoiding the lash base.

9. Be Cautious with Makeup Removal: When removing makeup, use oil-free makeup removers and be gentle around the eye area.

10. Consultation: If you notice any irritation or discomfort, please contact us for guidance and evaluation.
